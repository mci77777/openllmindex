# Morning Brew Newsletter — Policies

## Subscription Policy

- Free newsletters require a valid email address to subscribe.
- You may unsubscribe at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email.
- Premium memberships are billed monthly or annually and auto-renew until cancelled.
- Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period; no partial refunds are issued.
- A 7-day free trial is available for new Premium subscribers.

- Morning Brew content is © Morning Brew Inc. All rights reserved.

- Republishing full articles or newsletters in any form requires written permission from Morning Brew.

## Advertising Policy

- Free newsletters are ad-supported. Sponsored content is clearly labeled "Sponsored" or "Partner."
- Morning Brew does not sell personal subscriber data to advertisers.
- Advertisers are vetted for relevance and quality; Morning Brew reserves the right to decline any advertisement.

## Privacy & Data

- Subscriber email addresses and reading preferences are stored securely.
- Data is used to personalize content and measure engagement.
- Morning Brew does not sell personal data to third parties.
