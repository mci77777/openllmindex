# Morning Brew Newsletter — FAQ

## Getting Started

**How do I subscribe to Morning Brew?**
Visit https://morningbrew.com and enter your email address. You'll receive a confirmation email within minutes.

**Is Morning Brew free?**
Yes, the core daily newsletter and all vertical newsletters are free. A Premium tier is available for USD 9.99/month with additional benefits.

**How often is Morning Brew published?**
The flagship Daily newsletter is published every weekday (Monday–Friday). Vertical newsletters have their own schedules (see the Products page for details).

## Delivery & Technical

**Why isn't my newsletter arriving?**
Check your spam or promotions folder. Add team@morningbrew.com to your contacts to ensure delivery. If issues persist, contact support@morningbrew.com.

**Can I read past issues?**
Free subscribers can access the last 30 days of archives. Premium subscribers have access to the full archive.

**Is there a mobile app?**
Yes. Morning Brew is available on iOS and Android. Search "Morning Brew" in the App Store or Google Play.

## Premium Membership

**What does Premium include?**
Ad-free reading, full archive access, exclusive deep-dive reports, and early access to special editions.

**How do I cancel my Premium subscription?**
Log in to your account at https://morningbrew.com/account and select "Cancel Subscription." Your access continues until the end of the billing period.

**Do you offer a free trial?**
Yes, new Premium subscribers receive a 7-day free trial.

## Content & Advertising

**How does Morning Brew make money?**
Morning Brew is ad-supported. Sponsored content is clearly labeled in every issue.

**Can I contribute or pitch a story?**
We do not currently accept freelance pitches. For press inquiries, contact press@morningbrew.com.
