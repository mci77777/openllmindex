# Morning Brew Newsletter — About

## Who We Are

Morning Brew is a daily business newsletter that makes business news engaging and accessible. Founded in 2015 by Alex Lieberman and Austin Rief while they were students at the University of Michigan, Morning Brew has grown into one of the most widely read business newsletters in the United States.

## Our Mission

To make business and finance news enjoyable and easy to understand for the next generation of business leaders.

## Our Audience

- **Subscribers**: 4 million+ across all newsletters
- **Primary audience**: Young professionals aged 25–40
- **Topics covered**: Business, finance, technology, marketing, HR, and retail

## Our Newsletters

| Newsletter | Frequency | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| Morning Brew Daily | Weekdays | Business, finance, tech |
| Emerging Tech Brew | Weekly | AI, biotech, emerging tech |
| Marketing Brew | Daily | Marketing & advertising |
| HR Brew | Twice-weekly | Workplace & talent |
| Retail Brew | Weekdays | Retail & consumer trends |
