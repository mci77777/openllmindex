# The Golden Fork Restaurant — Policies

## Reservations

- Reservations are strongly recommended, especially for Friday–Sunday evenings.
- Book online at https://goldenfork.restaurant/reservations or call +1 (212) 555-0190.
- Reservations can be made up to 30 days in advance.
- We hold tables for 15 minutes past the reservation time; late arrivals may result in table release.
- For groups of 8 or more, please contact events@goldenfork.restaurant.
- A credit card is required to hold reservations for 6+ guests. Cancellations within 24 hours will incur a USD 25/person fee.

## Cancellation Policy

- Reservations for 1–5 guests: Cancel anytime, no charge.
- Reservations for 6+ guests: Cancel at least 24 hours in advance to avoid a USD 25/person fee.
- No-shows for 6+ guests will be charged the full cancellation fee.

## Takeout & Delivery

- Takeout orders can be placed online at https://goldenfork.restaurant/order or by phone.
- Takeout is available daily 5:00 PM – 9:30 PM.
- Delivery is available via DoorDash and Uber Eats within a 3-mile radius.
- A 15-minute pickup window is provided after order confirmation.
- We are unable to accommodate special dietary modifications on delivery orders.

## Dietary & Allergies

- Please inform your server of any food allergies or dietary requirements before ordering.
- While we take precautions, our kitchen handles nuts, gluten, dairy, shellfish, and other allergens.
- We cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free environment.
- Vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-conscious options are available — ask your server for details.

## Dress Code

- Smart casual attire is required in the dining room.
- Sportswear, beachwear, and athletic shoes are not permitted in the main dining room.
- The bar area has no dress code.

## Children & Private Events

- Children are welcome with adult supervision.
- Private dining room available for up to 20 guests. Contact events@goldenfork.restaurant for inquiries.

## Payment

- We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.
- Cash is accepted but not required.
- An 18% gratuity is automatically added for parties of 6 or more.
