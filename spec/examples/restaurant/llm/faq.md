# The Golden Fork Restaurant — FAQ

## Reservations & Seating

**Do I need a reservation?**
Walk-ins are welcome but we highly recommend reservations, especially Thursday–Sunday. Book at https://goldenfork.restaurant/reservations.

**How far in advance can I reserve?**
Up to 30 days in advance online. For special occasions, contact us directly at info@goldenfork.restaurant.

**Is there outdoor seating?**
Yes. We have a covered terrace with 20 seats, available weather permitting (April–October).

**Do you have a private dining room?**
Yes, our private dining room seats up to 20 guests. Contact events@goldenfork.restaurant for availability and pricing.

## Menu & Dietary

**Do you cater to dietary restrictions?**
Yes. Please inform your server of any allergies or requirements. We can accommodate most dietary needs with advance notice.

**Is your menu seasonal?**
Yes, select dishes change based on seasonal ingredient availability. Our full menu is available at https://goldenfork.restaurant/menu.

**Do you offer a tasting menu?**
We offer a 5-course chef's tasting menu for the full table on weekends. Ask your server for current selections.

**Is the restaurant vegetarian-friendly?**
Yes. We have several vegetarian options and can adapt some dishes — please ask your server.

## Takeout & Delivery

**Do you offer takeout?**
Yes, takeout is available daily from 5:00 PM – 9:30 PM. Order at https://goldenfork.restaurant/order.

**Do you deliver?**
Yes, via DoorDash and Uber Eats within 3 miles of the restaurant.

## Parking & Access

**Is parking available?**
We do not have a private parking lot. Street parking is available nearby. A valet service operates Thursday–Sunday evenings (USD 15).

**Is the restaurant wheelchair accessible?**
Yes, the restaurant is fully accessible. Please let us know in advance if you require specific accommodations.

## General

**Do you host large group events?**
Yes. Contact events@goldenfork.restaurant for groups of 8 or more.

**Is there a corkage fee?**
Yes, USD 25 per bottle if you bring your own wine.
