**What is TaskFlow?**
TaskFlow is a project management tool for teams. It helps you organize tasks, track progress, and collaborate in real time.

**Is there a free plan?**
Yes. The Free plan includes up to 5 projects, 3 team members, and 1 GB storage. No credit card required.

**How does billing work?**
Pro plans are billed per user per month. Annual billing saves 20%. Enterprise plans have custom pricing.

**Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?**
Yes, you can change plans at any time from Settings. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

**What integrations do you support?**
Slack, GitHub, GitLab, Jira, Google Calendar, Zapier, and a REST API for custom integrations.

**Is my data secure?**
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We are SOC 2 Type II certified.

**Can I export my data?**
Yes. Export all project data as JSON or CSV from Settings > Export at any time.

**Do you offer SSO?**
SSO via SAML 2.0 is available on Enterprise plans.

**How do I contact support?**
Free: Community forum. Pro: Priority email (support@taskflow.io). Enterprise: Dedicated account manager.
