# TaskFlow — Products

## Plans

### Free

- Up to 5 projects
- Up to 3 team members
- 1 GB storage
- Community support
- **Price**: Free forever

### Pro

- Unlimited projects
- Up to 20 team members
- 50 GB storage
- Priority email support
- Advanced analytics
- Custom workflows
- **Price**: $12/user/month (billed annually) or $15/user/month (billed monthly)

### Enterprise

- Everything in Pro
- Unlimited team members
- Unlimited storage
- Dedicated account manager
- SSO (SAML 2.0)
- Custom integrations
- SLA guarantee (99.9% uptime)
- **Price**: Contact sales
