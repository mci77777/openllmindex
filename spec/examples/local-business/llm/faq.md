# Sunrise Bakery — FAQ

**What are your hours?**
Mon–Fri 7 AM – 6 PM, Saturday 7 AM – 4 PM, Sunday 8 AM – 2 PM.

**Do you take custom cake orders?**
Yes! Custom cakes start at $45. Please order at least 48 hours in advance.

**Do you offer gluten-free options?**
We have a limited gluten-free selection available daily. Ask staff for current options.

**Do you deliver?**
We don't offer delivery at this time. All orders are pickup only.

**Can I place a catering order?**
Yes, we cater for events. Contact us at least 1 week in advance for catering orders of 20+ items.

**Do you sell coffee?**
Yes, we serve drip coffee, espresso, lattes, and tea. Beans are locally roasted by Portland Coffee Co.

**Is there parking?**
Free street parking available. A small lot is behind the building with 6 spaces.
