## Our Story

Sunrise Bakery has been serving the Oak Park neighborhood since 2015. We bake everything from scratch daily using locally sourced flour, butter, and seasonal ingredients.

## What We Offer

- Fresh breads (sourdough, whole wheat, rye, ciabatta)
- Pastries (croissants, pain au chocolat, cinnamon rolls)
- Cakes (custom and by the slice)
- Seasonal specialties
- Coffee and tea
