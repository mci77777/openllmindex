# Sunrise Bakery — Policies

## Store Hours

- **Monday – Friday**: 7:00 AM – 6:00 PM
- **Saturday**: 7:00 AM – 4:00 PM
- **Sunday**: 8:00 AM – 2:00 PM
- Closed on major holidays

## Orders & Pickup

- Walk-in orders welcome, no reservation needed
- Custom cake orders require 48 hours notice
- Large catering orders require 1 week notice
- Call (555) 234-5678 to place orders by phone

## Payment

- Cash, Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay
- No minimum purchase for card payments

## Allergens

- All products may contain wheat, dairy, eggs, tree nuts, and soy
- Gluten-free options available on request (limited selection)
- Please inform staff of any allergies before ordering
