# CraftHub Marketplace — FAQ

## For Buyers

**What is CraftHub?**
CraftHub is an online marketplace for handmade goods, vintage items, and craft supplies. Every item is sold by an independent maker or curator.

**How do I buy on CraftHub?**
Browse the marketplace at https://crafthub.io/shop, add items to your cart, and check out. You'll receive an order confirmation email, and the seller will ship directly to you.

**How do I track my order?**
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also view your order status at https://crafthub.io/orders.

**What if my item arrives damaged?**
Open a return case within 14 days of delivery via https://crafthub.io/orders. Provide photos of the damage. We'll work with the seller to issue a replacement or refund.

**Can I return a custom order?**
Custom and personalized items are non-returnable unless they arrive damaged or incorrect.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

**Do you ship internationally?**
Yes, many sellers ship internationally. Check each listing for shipping destinations. Buyers are responsible for customs duties and import taxes.

## For Sellers

**How do I become a seller on CraftHub?**
Create an account at https://crafthub.io/sell, complete identity verification, and set up your shop. Your first listing can go live within 24 hours of approval.

**What can I sell on CraftHub?**
Handmade items, vintage goods (20+ years old), and craft supplies. Mass-manufactured items are not permitted.

**What are the fees?**
- Listing fee: USD 0.20/item (4-month duration)
- Transaction fee: 6.5% of sale price
- Payment processing: 3% + USD 0.25 per transaction

**When do I get paid?**
Earnings are deposited weekly to your linked bank account. Minimum payout: USD 25.00.

**What if a buyer opens a dispute?**
Try to resolve it directly with the buyer. If unresolved, escalate to CraftHub's Resolution Center within 7 days.

## General

**Is CraftHub safe to use?**
Yes. All payments are processed securely. Buyer and seller identities are verified. Our Resolution Center protects both parties.
