# CraftHub Marketplace — Policies

## Buyer Policies

### Purchasing & Payment
- All prices are listed in USD. International buyers may see currency conversions at checkout.
- CraftHub accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.
- Payment is processed at the time of order. Orders are not confirmed until payment clears.

### Shipping
- Each seller ships independently. Estimated delivery times vary by seller and destination.
- Standard US shipping: 3–7 business days. Expedited options available from select sellers.
- International shipping is available from sellers who opt in. Buyers are responsible for customs duties and import taxes.
- A tracking number is provided once the seller ships.

### Returns & Refunds
- Buyers may request a return within 14 days of delivery for items that arrive damaged, incorrect, or not as described.
- Custom and personalized items are non-returnable unless they arrive damaged.
- To start a return, open a case through your order page at https://crafthub.io/orders.
- Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days after the seller confirms receipt.

### Disputes
- If a buyer and seller cannot resolve a dispute, CraftHub's Resolution Center will review the case.
- CraftHub's decision is final in all disputes.

## Seller Policies

### Eligibility
- Sellers must be 18 or older and have a verified PayPal or bank account.
- Products must be handmade, vintage (20+ years old), or craft supplies. Mass-manufactured items are prohibited.

### Fees
- Listing fee: USD 0.20 per item (active for 4 months or until sold).
- Transaction fee: 6.5% of item sale price (excluding shipping).
- Payment processing fee: 3% + USD 0.25 per transaction.

### Listing Requirements
- All listings must include at least one photo, a clear description, and accurate pricing.
- Sellers are responsible for accurate inventory and shipping estimates.
- Prohibited items include counterfeit goods, hazardous materials, and items that violate intellectual property rights.

### Payouts
- Seller earnings are deposited weekly to the linked bank account.
- Minimum payout threshold: USD 25.00.

## Platform Policies

### Privacy
- CraftHub collects buyer and seller data to facilitate transactions and improve the platform.
- Personal data is not sold to third parties. Full Privacy Policy: https://crafthub.io/privacy.

### Intellectual Property
- Sellers retain ownership of their product designs and photos.
- By listing on CraftHub, sellers grant CraftHub a license to display product images and descriptions for marketing purposes.

### Terms of Service
- Full Terms of Service: https://crafthub.io/terms
