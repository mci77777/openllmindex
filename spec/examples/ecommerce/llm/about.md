# ACME Outdoor Gear — About

## Our Story

Founded in 2018 in Portland, Oregon, ACME Outdoor Gear builds durable, affordable outdoor equipment for hikers, campers, and trail runners. We believe everyone deserves quality gear without the premium price tag.

## What We Sell

- Footwear (trail runners, hiking boots)
- Bags & packs (backpacks, dry bags)
- Clothing (jackets, base layers)
- Camping gear (tents, cookware)
- Accessories (trekking poles, headlamps, solar chargers)
