# MODA Fashion — About

## Our Story

MODA Fashion is a New York City–born DTC fashion label founded in **2019** to make premium, size-inclusive essentials feel effortless — elevated fabrics, clean silhouettes, and reliable fit across **Menswear**, **Womenswear**, and **Unisex**.

We build for the way people actually get dressed: repeatable outfits, pieces that travel, and staples that hold up in real life. Our drops focus on core wardrobe builders (tees, hoodies, dresses, leggings) and a curated line of sneakers and accessories.

## What We Sell

- **Menswear**: tees, hoodies, joggers, outerwear, underwear
- **Womenswear**: dresses, tops, leggings, bodysuits, outerwear
- **Unisex**: sneakers, bags, belts, caps, sunglasses

## Fit & Inclusivity

Fit is a product feature. We publish detailed size charts, model measurements, and fit notes on every product page. Many core styles are graded from **XXS–4XL**, with fabric and seam construction designed to feel supportive without feeling restrictive.

## Materials & Sustainability Commitments

MODA prioritizes durability and lower-impact materials where performance allows:

- **Recycled and responsible fibers**: we prioritize recycled synthetics and responsibly sourced natural fibers for core lines.
- **Longevity by design**: reinforced seams, colorfast dyes, and fabric testing to extend wear life.
- **Packaging**: right-sized mailers, paper-forward packaging, and reduced single-use plastics.
- **Shipping**: carbon-neutral shipping options at checkout (where available) and consolidated fulfillment to reduce emissions.

## Collaborations

We partner with creators and cultural voices on limited capsule drops — small runs, fast sell-through, and high-quality execution. Collaboration launches are announced on our social channels and email list.
