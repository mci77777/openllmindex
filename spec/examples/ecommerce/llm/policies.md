# MODA Fashion — Policies

This page summarizes MODA Fashion’s core customer policies for orders placed on https://modafashion.com.

## Shipping

### Processing time

- Orders typically process within **1–2 business days** (Mon–Fri, excluding holidays).
- During launches and peak periods, processing may take **3–5 business days**.

### Delivery options (United States)

- **Standard**: 3–6 business days — **Free** on orders **$100+**, otherwise calculated at checkout
- **Express**: **1–2 business days** — **$14.99**
- **Same-day (NYC & LA only)**: Available in select ZIP codes — fee shown at checkout

### International shipping

- We ship to **80+ countries**.
- **DHL Express**: typically **7–14 business days** depending on destination and customs processing.
- Duties/taxes: international customers are responsible for **customs duties, VAT, and import fees** unless stated otherwise at checkout.

### Tracking

- A tracking link is emailed when your order ships.
- Order status: https://modafashion.com/account/orders

## Returns & Exchanges

### Returns (30 days)

- **30-day free returns** from the delivery date for eligible items shipped within the US (prepaid return label provided).
- Items must be **unworn, unwashed, and in resellable condition** with original tags/packaging.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method after inspection (typically **3–7 business days**).

Start a return: https://modafashion.com/returns

### Exchanges (45 days)

- Size/color exchanges are available within **45 days** of delivery, subject to inventory.
- Exchange requests are processed once the original item is received or per the instructions in the return portal.

### Non-returnable items

Unless required by law, the following are generally not eligible for return:

- Final-sale items
- Gift cards
- Items marked as hygiene-sensitive (e.g., certain underwear) if opened/used

## Pre-orders

- Items labeled **Pre-order** ship on the estimated ship date shown on the product page and at checkout.
- If your order contains both in-stock and pre-order items, we may ship together unless you select split shipping (if available).

## 1-Year Quality Guarantee

- MODA offers a **1-year quality guarantee** on manufacturing defects (materials/workmanship) from the delivery date.
- This does not cover normal wear and tear, accidental damage, improper care, or alterations by third parties.

To file a claim: support@modafashion.com (include order number, photos, and a description of the issue).

## Payment & Billing

### Accepted payment methods

- Major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- PayPal
- Apple Pay, Google Pay, Shop Pay
- Buy Now, Pay Later: **Affirm** and **Klarna** (where available)

### Currency, taxes, and pricing

- Prices are shown in **USD** unless otherwise indicated.
- Sales tax (where applicable) is calculated at checkout.
- Promotions cannot always be applied retroactively.

## Privacy & Data (GDPR + CCPA)

- We collect personal data to process orders, provide support, and improve the shopping experience.
- We do **not** sell personal information.
- Privacy Policy: https://modafashion.com/privacy
- Terms of Service: https://modafashion.com/terms

Data requests (access/deletion): privacy@modafashion.com

- Size charts and fit notes are published per product.

## Sustainability

MODA’s sustainability commitments focus on measurable operations and transparency:

- **Materials**: prioritize recycled and responsible fibers for core styles where performance allows.
- **Packaging**: recyclable, paper-forward packaging; reduced single-use plastics.
- **Shipping**: carbon-neutral shipping options at checkout (where available) via verified offsets.

Learn more: https://modafashion.com/sustainability
