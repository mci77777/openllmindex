# ACME Outdoor Gear — Policies

## Shipping

- **Standard Shipping**: 5–7 business days, free on orders over $75
- **Express Shipping**: 2–3 business days, $12.99
- **International**: 10–15 business days, rates calculated at checkout
- Ships from Portland, OR, USA

## Returns

- **30-day return window** from delivery date
- Items must be unused and in original packaging
- Free return shipping on defective items
- Refund processed within 5 business days of receiving return

## Warranty

- All ACME-branded products carry a **2-year limited warranty**
- Covers manufacturing defects, not normal wear
- Contact support@acme-outdoor.com with proof of purchase

## Payment

- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- All prices in USD, tax calculated at checkout
